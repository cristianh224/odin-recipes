Strawberry Blueberry Smoothie

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1
Jump to Nutrition Facts

Description

Indulge in a burst of vibrant flavors with this refreshing strawberry blueberry smoothie. Packed with antioxidant-rich berries and creamy yogurt, it's the perfect blend of sweet and tangy goodness. Start your day right or enjoy it as a guilt-free treat anytime!/p>

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup almond milk
  • 1⁄2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1⁄2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1⁄2 cup low-fat plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon flax seed oil
  • 1 teaspoon agave nectar

ONE Simple Step!

  1. Blend almond milk, strawberries, blueberries, yogurt, flax seed oil, and agave nectar in a blender until smooth.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

231 calories, 5g fat, 40g carbs, 9g protein
